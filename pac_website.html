About Us

Who We Are

Purdue Aviation Consulting is a premier student-run consulting organization dedicated to advancing the aviation industry through innovative research, strategic analysis, and practical solutions.

Our team of passionate Purdue students combines academic excellence with real-world aviation expertise to deliver impactful consulting services to clients ranging from airlines to aerospace manufacturers.

We're committed to developing the next generation of aviation leaders while providing tangible value to our industry partners.
